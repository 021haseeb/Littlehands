Frequently Asked Questions

?

What is the admission process?

Visit the school or contact us on WhatsApp, submit the admission form, and complete the assessment process (KG onwards).

Is there an admission test?

Yes, an admission test is mandatory for admissions from KG onwards.

What is the admission test fee?

The admission test fee is Rs. 1,000/-.

How can I get the admission test syllabus?

You can collect it from the school office or request it via WhatsApp.

What is the minimum age for admission?

Playgroup admissions start from 2 years of age.

What classes do you offer?

We offer classes from Playgroup to Matric and O Levels.

What are the school timings?

Montessori: 8:45 AM – 12:00 PM
Primary & Secondary: 7:50 AM – 1:20 PM.

What curriculum do you follow?

We follow a conceptual and activity-based curriculum aligned with Sindh Board and Cambridge standards.

What co-curricular activities do you offer?

We offer Taekwondo, Computer & AI classes, Gardening, Art, Public Speaking, and Sports activities.

Do you provide transport facilities?

Please contact the school office to check transport availability in your area.

How can parents track their child's progress?

Parents can monitor attendance, homework, announcements, and fee records through our online parent portal.

How can I contact the school?

You can visit our campus, call us, or send us a message on WhatsApp for any queries.
