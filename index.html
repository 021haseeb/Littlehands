Frequently Asked Questions

?

What is the age group for admission?

We welcome children from 2 to 6 years old for our playgroup, nursery, LKG and UKG programs.

What are the school timings?

Our standard school timings are Monday to Friday, from 8:30 AM to 12:30 PM. Extracurricular schedules are shared separately.

What facilities does the school provide?

We offer fully CCTV-monitored safe play areas, multimedia classrooms, digital activity zones, an internal library, and clean medical aids.

How can I enroll my child?

You can fill out the online form via the 'Enroll Now' button or visit our campus registry to collect the verification packet.
